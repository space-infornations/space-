Historic Space Missions

Apollo 11 Moon Landing

1969

NASA's Apollo 11 was the first manned mission to land on the Moon. Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin were the first humans to walk on the lunar surface.

Viking 1 Mars Landing

1976

Viking 1 was the first successful mission to land a spacecraft on Mars. It performed experiments to look for signs of life.

Hubble Space Telescope Launch

1990

The Hubble Space Telescope was launched into low Earth orbit. It has since become one of the most important instruments in the history of astronomy.

International Space Station

2000

The first crew arrived at the International Space Station (ISS). The ISS is a modular space station in low Earth orbit.
